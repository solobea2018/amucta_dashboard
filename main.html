Admission into the Diploma in Early Childhood Development with Special Needs Education requires one of the following qualifications:

Advanced Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (ACSEE):
  1. An applicant must have passed with Division I, II, or III with two Principal Passes.
Certificate in Education:
  1. Certificate of Primary School Teacher Education, OR
  2. Certificate in Early Childhood Education from a recognized institution.
